[awesome-ai2-thor](https://github.com/XUJay111/awesome-ai2-thor) is a curated collection of resources for AI2-THOR and embodied AI research.

The project is intended to help researchers and engineers quickly find relevant papers, environments, tasks, tools, and codebases for building interactive agents in simulated 3D scenes.

Key directions covered by the repository include:

- embodied AI environments and simulators;
- visual navigation, interaction, and manipulation;
- 3D scene understanding and language-grounded agents;
- practical resources for working with AI2-THOR.
